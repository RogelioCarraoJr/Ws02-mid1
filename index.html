I Am The Fated Villain

About Gu Changge

Gu Changge was from the start a transmigrator, but due to his Devil Heart, he was overwhelmed by his demonic nature.It was only at 21 years old (the start of the novel) that he recovered his past life memories and consciousness.

Gu Changge's Characteristics

  1. He has a slender, tall body and jet black hair.
  2. His bearing is often described as elegant and lofty, yet cold and aloof to those extremely familiar with him.
  3. He is a scheming and sadistic man who takes pleasure in the misfortune, pain and despair of his enemies.
